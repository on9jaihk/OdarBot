`on9!addrole` **Gives someone a role** __Usage__: *!addrole <@user> <role>*
`on9!removerole` **Removes someone's role** __Usage__: *!removerole <@user> <role>*
`on9!mute` **Mutes a user** __Usage__: *!mute <@user> <time>*
`on9!unmute` ** Unmutes a user** __Usage__: *!unmute <@user>*
`on9!kick` **Kicks a user from the server** __Usage__: *!kick <@user> <reason>*
`on9!ban` **Bans a user from the server** __Usage__: *!ban <@user> <reason>*
`on9!clear` **Clears the chat** __Usage__: *!clear <amount>*
